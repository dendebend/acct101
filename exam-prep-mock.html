1 Unit 02A: 5 Account Types & The Accounting Equation
1. Which one of the following is NOT a revenue account?
Unearned Revenue is a LIABILITY - money received BEFORE services are performed.
2. Which of the following is NOT an expense account?
Prepaid Expense is an ASSET - expense paid in advance that hasn't been used yet.
3. If total liabilities decreased by $30,000 during a period and stockholders' equity increased by $35,000 during the same period, the amount and direction of the period's change in total assets is a:
A = L + E. If L decreases by 30K and E increases by 35K, then A changes by -30K + 35K = +5K
4. A corporation with total stockholders' equity of $75,000 paid a $12,000 business debt. As a result of this transaction, total stockholders' equity:
Paying a debt: Cash (A) decreases, Liability (L) decreases. A = L + E stays balanced. E doesn't change!
5. Tatum Enterprises' total assets decreased by $11,000 during the year. Its common stock increased by $12,000 during the same period. Net income totaled $8,000 during the year. What occurred to the company's total liabilities during the year?
A = L + E. E increased by 12K (stock) + 8K (NI) = 20K. A decreased by 11K. So L = A - E = -11K - 20K = -31K decrease

25. Accounts Receivable was $18,500 at the beginning of the month. Sales on account totaled $209,000 during the month. If Accounts Receivable was $53,500 at the end of the month, how much were customer collections during the month?
Beginning A/R + Sales - Collections = Ending A/R. So: 18,500 + 209,000 - Collections = 53,500
26. Accounts Payable was $4,300 at the beginning of the month. Payments on account totaled $21,000 during the month. If Accounts Payable was $33,300 at the end of the month, how much were purchases on account during the month?
Beginning A/P + Purchases - Payments = Ending A/P. So: 4,300 + Purchases - 21,000 = 33,300
27. Cash amounted to $5,200 at the end of the month. Cash receipts and cash disbursements were $9,700 and $14,000, respectively, during the month. What is the balance of cash at the beginning of the month?

36. Saira's Maid Service began the year with total assets of $120,000 and stockholders' equity of $40,000. During the year the company earned $90,000 in net income and paid $20,000 in dividends. Total assets at the end of the year were $215,000. Total liabilities at the end of the year were:
Ending E = 40,000 + 90,000 - 20,000 = 110,000. A = L + E, so 215,000 = L + 110,000
37. Barken Company's records show: Beginning RE = $400,000, Ending RE = $500,000, Total Revenues = $670,000, Total Dividends = $40,000. Total expenses for January were:
First find NI: 400,000 + NI - 40,000 = 500,000, so NI = 140,000. Then: Revenue - Expenses = NI, so 670,000 - Expenses = 140,000
