3. Green Leaf Dispensary's total liabilities decreased by $30,000 during a period (paid off grow equipment loans) and stockholders' equity increased by $35,000 during the same period. The amount and direction of the period's change in total assets is a:

4. High Times Cannabis Co. with total stockholders' equity of $75,000 paid a $12,000 debt to their hydroponic supplier. As a result of this transaction, total stockholders' equity:
Paying a debt: Cash (A) decreases, Liability (L) decreases. A = L + E stays balanced. E doesn't change!
5. Bud Brothers Dispensary's total assets decreased by $11,000 during the year (sold some old vaporizers). Its common stock increased by $12,000 during the same period. Net income from selling premium strains totaled $8,000 during the year. What occurred to the company's total liabilities during the year?
A = L + E. E increased by 12K (stock) + 8K (NI) = 20K. A decreased by 11K. So L = A - E = -11K - 20K = -31K decrease
